We're excited to introduce Actenda, a new kind of productivity app for iOS that challenges you to stop pretending you're productive and start building routines that deliver real results. Actenda helps you set big goals, create meaningful daily actions, and plan your schedule directly in your native calendar. Every action is linked to a goal and measurable output, so you can track genuine progress, not just busywork.

Key Features:

  • Morning resolution & evening review for daily focus and reflection
  • Goal management with aspirational, present-tense outcomes
  • Hour-by-hour schedule planning with native calendar sync
  • Daily action tracking and streak analytics
  • 30+ productivity templates inspired by famous routines
  • Action-oriented daily challenges across 8 growth categories
  • Full offline functionality, accessibility, and dark mode
